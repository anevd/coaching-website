WHAT CAN I GIVE AS A COACH

Depending on your request, we will put together a set of tools
for growth and development that is right for you.

Together we will reveal your unlimited potential, focus on your
dreams, and draw up an effective plan for their
implementation.

After my coaching sessions, you will get rid of the fear of
change, you will understand that you can manage the events in
your life and do what you have long dreamed of. To live the way
you want is simple, the main thing is to correctly prioritize and
choose your right path!

WHO DO I WORK WITH

Most of my interest is directed to working with life requests, that is, with topics
that are important to you in life:

changing patterns of behavior and reactions, identifying limiting beliefs and
changing them, helping in self-acceptance;

search, setting goals in life and the beginning of movement towards them,
the question of finding and choosing one's vocation, working out attitudes
towards money;

harmonization of relations with parents, work with grievances and their
development, anger management, disclosure of femininity.
