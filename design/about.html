And a number of difficult situations in my life, through which, I am sure, each of us goes through. But I can safely say that thanks to coaching, regular therapy and my constant self-development, I managed to find my vocation, overcome difficulties in an environmentally friendly way, and realize myself as a woman, wife and mother.
